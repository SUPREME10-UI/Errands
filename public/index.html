FAQ

Frequently Answered Queries

Quick answers regarding our operational zones, insurance guarantees, and custom packages.

What geographical areas do you cover?
Currently, our primary courier coverage zone includes the entire Greater Accra Metropolitan Area (including East Legon, Airport Residential, Cantonments, Dzorwulu, Tema, Spintex, Oyarifa, and surrounding areas). For Site Inspection Updates, we also cover Kumasi and Koforidua.
Are my packages and bank deposits insured?
Yes. Every RME order is backed by premium logistical insurance up to GHS 10,000 for valuables and documents. Bank runs and cash-in-transit are handled with dedicated safety checks, strict dual-signature validation, and real-time bank slip uploads.
How quickly are Urgent and Express errands handled?
Express errands are dispatched immediately with dedicated direct couriers (no multi-stops) and are guaranteed to be completed within 60-90 minutes. Urgent errands are completed within 3 hours. Standard errands are processed within 1 business day.
